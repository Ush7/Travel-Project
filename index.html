Why Windsor?

Windsor, the border city

Windsor located on the shore of the Detroit River across the Ambassador Bridge is a hub to automotive industy, creative innovation and diverse culinary experience. Windsor played a vital role in the Underground Railroad, antislavery freedom movement. The city's history is preserved in local museums and annual cultural events.

Today, Windsor is a multicultural marvel, serving as a home to a diverse mix of immigrant communities that showcase their culture predominantly through food. Windsor is home to the Hiram Walker and Sons Ltd. Distillery, which boasts a fascinating history in Canadian whisky production. Furthermore, the region features beautiful parks and gardens, all situated along its picturesque waterfront.
